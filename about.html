Members

Alexander James (A.J) McLean

Born January 9th, 1978

He is of Cuban and German ancestry on his mother's side and Scottish, Irish and English on his father's side. He's an only child because his twin sisters died during childbirth. He was raised by his mother and grandparents (Ursula and Adolph) as his parents divorced when he was just two years old. The divorce affected him deeply, causing him to become angry, defensive, troubled and frustrated.


Howard Dwaine (Howie D.) Dorough

Born August 22, 1973

He is the youngest of five siblings, older brother, John, and three sisters, Pollyanna, also a singer, Angela "Angie" and Caroline, who died from lupus in 1998.


Nicholas Gene (Nick Carter)

Born January 28, 1980

Carter began his acting and singing career at a young age, when his mother enrolled him in voice lessons and dance lessons in ballet and tap at Karl and DiMarco's School of Theatre and Dance when he was 10. He performed in several commercials, such as the Florida State Lottery and The Money Store. He played the lead role in the fourth grade production of Phantom of the Opera at Miles Elementary School.


Kevin Scott Richardson

Born October 3, 1971

Growing up, Kevin lived on a 10-hectare farm with his parents and two older brothers (Jerald Wayne Jr. and Tim), one of whom is a model, for 9 years and later lived in a log cabin at Cathedral Domain Camp, a church-owned youth camp which his father ran, until he was 18


Brian Litrell

Born February 20, 1975

Growing up, Littrell played in both Little League and the Babe Ruth League. He first started to sing in the choir of Porter Memorial Baptist Church in Lexington, and later started singing in his high school choir and acting in the school production of Grease as Roger at Tates Creek High School
